Common Apple Diseases & Pests in Kashmir

Tap any disease card to learn symptoms, prevention & treatment

Apple Scab
Fungal · High Risk
Olive-green to brown spots on leaves & fruit. Most destructive disease in Kashmir orchards. Peak risk April–May.
Tap to learn more
Powdery Mildew
Fungal · Medium Risk
White powdery coating on new shoots & buds. Stunts growth and reduces yield significantly.
Tap to learn more
Marssonina Leaf Blotch
Fungal
Dark brown irregular spots leading to premature leaf drop and weakened trees.
Tap to learn more
Fire Blight
Bacterial · Critical
Shoots turn brown & wilt like burnt. Spreads explosively in warm humid conditions.
Tap to learn more
European Red Mite
Mite · High Risk
Tiny red mites on leaf undersides causing bronzing. Severe in hot dry spells June–August.
Tap to learn more
Two-Spotted Spider Mite
Mite
Fine silky webbing on leaf undersides. Causes yellowing and defoliation in summer.
Tap to learn more
Bitter Rot / Black Rot
Fungal
Sunken brown lesions on fruit starting from calyx end. Makes fruit unmarketable.
Tap to learn more
Alternaria Leaf Blotch
Fungal
Concentric target-like spots on leaves. Causes early defoliation and reduced photosynthesis.

🗓️ SKUAST-K 2026 Spray Schedule

Apple (Kashmir Valley) · Phenology-based · Active stage highlighted

❄️ Dormant Stage (Dec – Mid Mar)
Mineral Oil 2L + Copper Oxychloride 300g / 100L
🌱 Silver Tip Stage (Mid Mar)
Copper Oxychloride 300g / 100L
🌿 Green Tip Stage (Early Apr)
Mancozeb 300g OR Captan 300g / 100L
🍃 Half-Inch Green Stage (10–12 Apr)
Mancozeb 300g + Imidacloprid 20ml / 100L
🌸 Pink Bud Stage (13–19 Apr)
Difenoconazole 30ml + Imidacloprid 20ml / 100L
🌺 Full Pink / Balloon Stage (20–24 Apr)
Difenoconazole 30ml + Spiromesifen 40ml / 100L
🌼 Full Bloom Stage (Late Apr)
⚠️ Avoid spraying — protect pollinators
🍂 Petal Fall (Late Apr – Early May)
Carbendazim 100g + Chlorpyrifos 150ml / 100L
🍏 Fruit Set Stage (May)
Hexaconazole 100ml + Abamectin 50ml / 100L
🍎 Fruit Development (May–Jun)
Hexaconazole 100ml OR Myclobutanil 40g / 100L
🕷️ Mite Control Period (July)
Abamectin 50ml OR Spiromesifen 40ml / 100L
🧺 Pre-Harvest Stage (Aug – Mid Sep)
Captan 200g / 100L · Observe 14-day PHI
🏡 Harvest & Post-Harvest (Sep–Nov)
No sprays · Foliar Urea 2% after leaf fall
⚠️ Disclaimer: This is a reference guide based on SKUAST-K recommendations. Actual dosage may vary by variety, weather and orchard history. Always consult our team before application.
